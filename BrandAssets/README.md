# EEAccess Brand Assets

The app icon and logo use an `EE` monogram from **Elbaeverywhere** and a small
barcode motif to connect the brand to the app's scannable-card wallet purpose.

## Files

- `eeaccess-app-icon.svg` — editable source for the iOS/watchOS app icon.
- `eeaccess-logo-lockup.svg` — horizontal logo lockup for marketing, App Store
  screenshots, and documentation.
- `eeaccess-app-icon-1024.png` — rendered app icon source used by both asset catalogs.
- `eeaccess-logo-lockup.png` — rendered preview/export of the logo lockup.

## Direction

- Monogram: bold `EE`, readable at small sizes.
- Product cue: barcode stripes for loyalty cards, gym passes, and QR/barcode use.
- Palette: deep navy base, cyan/blue card gradient, warm yellow accent.
- Shape: rounded digital card, suitable for iOS and Apple Watch icon masks.
