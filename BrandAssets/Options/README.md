# EEAccess Brand Options

All four options adapt the EEFitness brandbook to EEAccess:

- `#001A24` deep navy background
- `#0C241C` pine green
- `#0F3324` teal
- `#22C55E` mint green accent
- `#FFFFFF` white
- Bold geometric sans-serif wordmark treatment

## Options

1. `option-1-card-pass` — tilted digital card with `EE` monogram and barcode.
2. `option-2-watch-qr` — compact watch-app feel with QR dots.
3. `option-3-stacked-wallet` — stacked-card wallet metaphor.
4. `option-4-scan-access` — simple scan/access mark with forward motion.
